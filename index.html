Tourism in sri lanka

Foreign tourism has significantly impacted three renowned Sri Lankan destinations. Mirissa's coastal charm and water activities attract travelers, boosting its economy. Sigiriya's historical rock fortress draws tourists, leading to the growth of hospitality services and aiding conservation. The Temple of the Tooth in Kandy, a UNESCO site, benefits from tourism, supporting the local economy and cultural awareness, while considerations of sustainability and overtourism persist across these locales.

Mirissa

Foreign tourism transformed Mirissa into a sought-after beach destination, boosting the economy with water sports, vibrant nightlife, and whale watching.
